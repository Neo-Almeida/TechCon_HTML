Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for developers in San Francisco. Over the years, it has evolved into a global tech event attended by thousands, showcasing innovations from AI to cybersecurity.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to empowering the global tech community through knowledge sharing, hands-on experience, and building connections that shape the future of technology.
